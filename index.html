I am a graduate student in computer science at Tampere University, where I mainly study topics of machine learning and math around it. In particular, I am interested in deep learning methods and computer vision.

I maintain a blog on topics revolving around machine learning and related stuff. The blog posts are for me to consolidate my knowledge, and hopefully for some of you to get insights and have good reading experiences. To start this blog, I was inspired by people like Gregory Gundersen, Andrej Karpathy and Christopher Olah.

Aside from computers and machine learning, I enjoy reading, music and sports. Especially ball games such as ice hockey and tennis are close to my heart. I am also particularly interested in electric vehicles and their technology.

Thanks to Gregory Gundersen for sharing this minimalistic theme.
